Change of Department
(rules & procedures)

Programme Ph.D. Integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D.
Time of Change ONLY at the end of Semester I ONLY at the end of Semester III
Minimum Requirements
  1. CPI in top 20% of batch (see below)
  2. Consent of prospective Supervisor
  3. Consent of prospective Department
  1. CPI in top 30% of batch (see below)
  2. Consent of prospective Supervisor
  3. Consent of prospective Department
For consent of the prospective Department, Department-specific performance criteria will also apply.

Calculation of CPI for a Batch

Ph.D. Program Integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D. Program
Only students who are continuing in the TIFR Graduate School after 1 semester are considered. Only students who are continuing in the TIFR Graduate School after 3 semesters are considered.
Each of the students in question must have completed at least 20 Credits. Each of the students in question must have completed at least 60 Credits.
Project and Reading Course Grades are not counted for the CPI calculation, but they will be counted among the Credits in the previous item. Project and Reading Course Grades are not counted for the CPI calculation, but they will be counted among the Credits in the previous item.
Fractional numbers for the top 20% shall NOT be rounded off to the nearest integer. For example, if a batch has 9 students, then only the top student will be permitted to change, and not the 2nd student. Fractional numbers for the top 30% shall NOT be rounded off to the nearest integer. For example, if a batch has 9 students, then only the top 2 students will be permitted to change, and not the 3rd student.
CPI values shall be rounded off to the first decimal place. In case of a tie, the maximum benefit may be allowed to the tied students. CPI values shall be rounded off to the first decimal place. In case of a tie, the maximum benefit may be allowed to the tied students.
