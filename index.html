Subject Board of Physics

The Subject Board for Physics (SBP) is responsible for running and monitoring the graduate programme in Physics across TIFR. All graduate students in the Physics Departments under the School of Natural Sciences as well as the National Centre for Radio Astrophysics (NCRA), the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS) and the TIFR Centre for Interdisciplinary Sciences (TCIS) are governed by the SBP rules and procedures.
